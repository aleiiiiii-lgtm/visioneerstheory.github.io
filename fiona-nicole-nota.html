Fiona Nicole Nota

My name is Fiona Nicole Nota, and I was born and raised in Pasig City. I come from a family that constantly supports and inspires me to do my best in everything I pursue. Outside of school, I enjoy singing and playing badminton. These hobbies allow me to balance both creativity and physical activity, which help me express myself and maintain a healthy lifestyle.

I chose the ABM strand because I would like to have my own business someday. I believe ABM is the right track for me since it focuses on business, management, and leadership skills that I know will be valuable in the future. Through this strand, I am learning how to be independent, responsible, and more prepared for the career I want to achieve.

If I were not in ABM, my wildest dream would be to explore a different path, but no matter what, I would still want to pursue something that challenges me and helps me grow. Deep down, I know that being an entrepreneur is where my passion lies, and it is the goal I will continue to work for. In the end, my journey is about striving to build a life filled with purpose, success, and inspiration for others.
